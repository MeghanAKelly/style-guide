About

The Fonts

The typography is taken from the Elsevier Style Guide - Nexus Sans Pro and Nexus Serif Pro. The fallback fonts are Arial and Georgia, respectively. Additional information can be found in the Elsevier Style Guide.

Getting the fonts

The fonts have to be requested from central Elsevier. To request the fonts:

  • Go to Non Solus.
  • Click on "Tools & Resources" in the top menu.
  • Click on "Branding" under "Communications Toolset".
  • On the right, under "Quick Links," click "Request Nexus font."

Once you request it, it will take a few days for you to be sent an email with the fonts.

Composition

Headers

  • Header 1 is 28px.
  • Header 2 is 24px.
  • Header 3 is 20px.
  • Body copy is 20px.

Text and link colors

There are many exceptions, but in general, text and links follow the pattern below.


White Background

Regular text that is on a white background will almost always be #737373.

Links that are on a white background will almost always be #007398.


Dark Background

Regular text and links that are on a white background will almost always be white.

Links that are pale blue on a dark background are designed to stand out and are #91ddf2.

Behavior

Hover States

All links should have hover states. If the text is white, the hover state will either be #91ddf2 or #004e65.
